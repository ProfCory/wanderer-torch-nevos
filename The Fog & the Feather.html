Common & Uncommon Underground Travel Gear

Shelter & Rest

  • Folded Burrow Tent (Common)

    A tarp-like cocoon that uses spring ribs and ground spikes to form a quick sleeping nook in tight tunnels. Canvas is reinforced against humidity and mold.

    Price: 10 gp | Weight: 6 lbs

  • Stonebiter Stakes (Common)

    Iron pitons with mild enchantment to anchor to stone. Used for tents, ropes, or wall hammocks.

    Price: 5 gp for 6 | Effect: Auto-anchors to rock, DC 20 to pull free.

  • Echo-Damp Bedroll (Uncommon)

    Bedroll that muffles sound within 10 ft when sealed. Used by Veilspire scribe-guards and quiet thieves alike.

    Price: 40 gp | Effect: Grants advantage on stealth during rests underground.

  • Cave-Dome Frame (Uncommon)

    Collapsible half-sphere frame (like a yurt) that assembles with no nails. Wrap it in your own cloth/tarp. Popular with residuum miners and archivists.

    Price: 35 gp (frame only), 60 gp (with dust-proof veil tarp) | Bonus: +2 to saves vs inhaled dust or mold while inside.

Survival & Navigation

  • Veinscripter Chalk (Common)

    Glows faintly when drawn along walls in active cave systems. Fades after 8 hours.

    Price: 2 gp per stick | Uses: 10 marks per stick.

  • Mosswater Canteen (Uncommon)

    Contains an alchemical lining that purifies stagnant or fungus-heavy water over 1 hour.

    Price: 25 gp | Effect: Removes minor toxins, spores, and parasites.

  • Lantern of the Slow Fade (Uncommon)

    Emits no visible flame or smoke. Instead produces a ghostly pale light in a 30 ft cone that dims over 10 minutes if not tended.

    Price: 50 gp | Fuel: Wisp oil or fungal wax (harder to find than normal oil).

  • Tunnel Censer (Uncommon)

    Releases a low-burning incense designed to deter common cave vermin and subtly mark routes (detectable by trained noses).

    Price: 30 gp | Duration: 2 hours | Smell trail lasts 12 hrs

Beast & Burden

  • Pack-Bug Shell (Common)
